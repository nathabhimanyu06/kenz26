About BrahMos II

BrahMos II is an upcoming hypersonic cruise missile currently under joint development by India and Russia. Capable of reaching speeds exceeding Mach 7, it is designed for both land-attack and naval missions, offering unparalleled speed and precision.

Features

  • Hypersonic Speed: Capable of reaching speeds exceeding Mach 7.
  • Precision Targeting: Equipped with state-of-the-art guidance systems for pinpoint accuracy.
  • Multi-Platform Compatibility: Can be launched from land, sea, or air-based platforms.
  • Enhanced Range: Extended range compared to its predecessors.

History

BrahMos II is the successor to the highly successful BrahMos missile system. Initiated in the early 2010s, this collaborative project between India and Russia aims to redefine modern warfare with hypersonic capabilities. The program is in advanced stages of research and testing.
