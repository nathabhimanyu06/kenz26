About RudraM-II

The RudraM-II is an advanced air-launched missile developed by India. Designed to neutralize enemy radar systems and air defense mechanisms, it is part of India's indigenous weapons program under DRDO. With its precision targeting and extended range, RudraM-II is an essential component of India's defense capabilities.

Features

  • Advanced Seeker: Equipped with an advanced seeker for pinpoint accuracy against radar targets.
  • Long Range: Capable of engaging targets at extended ranges, ensuring operational superiority.
  • Multiple Launch Platforms: Compatible with a wide range of fighter jets in the Indian Air Force fleet.
  • Electronic Counter-Countermeasures (ECCM): Designed to operate in heavily jammed environments.

History

Development of the Rudram series began in the early 2010s, with RudraM-II being unveiled as a major leap in India's missile capabilities. It is a key element of India's strategy to maintain air dominance and deter enemy defenses. The project showcases India's growing expertise in defense technology and self-reliance in weapon development.
