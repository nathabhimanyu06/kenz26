About Nirbhay

The Nirbhay Missile is India's long-range, subsonic cruise missile designed for precision targeting and versatility. Developed by DRDO, this missile is capable of carrying nuclear warheads and is engineered for both land and sea-based deployment.

Features

  • Long Range: Capable of engaging targets over 1,000 km away.
  • Subsonic Speed: Operates at a speed of 0.7 Mach for stealth and precision.
  • Terrain Hugging: Low-altitude flight path to evade radar detection.
  • Multiple Payloads: Designed to carry nuclear and conventional warheads.

History

The Nirbhay Missile was first tested in 2013 as part of India's advanced missile development program. Since its inception, it has undergone numerous successful tests to improve reliability and performance, making it a critical component of India's defense strategy.
