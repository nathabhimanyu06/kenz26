About SpaDeX

The Space Docking Experiment (SpaDeX) is a pioneering mission aimed at demonstrating advanced docking and rendezvous capabilities in space. Developed by ISRO, this experiment focuses on validating critical technologies required for autonomous docking, a key capability for future manned space missions and orbital assembly.

Key Objectives

  • Demonstrate autonomous docking and undocking in orbit.
  • Test rendezvous sensors, algorithms, and navigation systems.
  • Develop technologies for on-orbit servicing and satellite maintenance.
  • Lay the groundwork for future space station modules and interplanetary missions.
